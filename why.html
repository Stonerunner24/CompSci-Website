Why be a Computer Science Major?

The field of Computer Science is an ever growing and changing field. Computer Science offers the opprotunity to join a work force where you will be constantly learning and growing through your entire professional career.

The classes that are offered as part of a degree in Computer Science are some of the most challenging yet rewarding classes that a college student can take. From the basics that are taught in Programming I, to Human Computer Interaction and Software Engineering V, you will never be bored in this degree.

There are many careers that are open to you with a degree in Computer Science. Some of these include:
  • Software engineer
  • Web Developer
  • Mobile app Developer
  • Ai Engineer
  • And many more
